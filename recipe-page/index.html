Chicken Fried Rice

This chicken fried rice recipe is a quick and delicious meal made with rice, vegetables, eggs, and seasoned chicken. It is perfect for a simple homemade dinner.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 1 cup diced chicken
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil

Instructions

  1. Cook the chicken in a pan until fully cooked.
  2. Add vegetables and cook until they become tender.
  3. Add the rice and stir everything together.
  4. Mix in the eggs and soy sauce until everything is combined.
  5. Serve the fried rice warm and enjoy.
